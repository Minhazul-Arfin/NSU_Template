UNDERGRADE PROGRAMS
Minimum Qualifications for Admission Test
Academic Qualifications:
  1. Combined GPA of 8.0 in both SSC and HSC with a minimum GPA of 3.5 in each

    2. OR

  2. GCE O-Levels and A-Levels: O-Levels in five subjects with a minimum Average Grade Point of 2.5 and A-Levels in two subjects with a minimum Average Grade Point of 2.0. Only one ‘E’ Grade is acceptable in combined O & A-Levels. (Scale A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2 & E=1)

    3. OR

  3. US High School Diploma, IB Diploma Program (IB-DP) or equivalent
  4. Students with academic qualifications other than the above need to contact Admissions Office to check their eligibility.
  5. Foreign Applicants: candidates must have a minimum avg 60% marks in Grades 10th and 12th.

Please read carefully:

  1. Please check the eligibility criteria before applying. Applicants not meet the eligibility criteria may be refused admission and payments made for the same will not be refunded
  2. Candidates whose first choice is LLB or BPharm will be given first preference


Specific Programs Requirements
  1. For all Engineering programs (BS in CSE, EEE, and CEE) of the School of Engineering & Physical Sciences, candidates must have -
    1. Math and Physics with a minimum B grade in HSC

      2. OR

    2. Math & Physics with a minimum C & E grade respectively in A-Levels

      3. OR

    3. Math and Physics with a minimum C grade in O-Levels AND must have Math or Physics with a minimum C grade in A-Levels to be eligible to apply.

    OR

  2. GCE O-Levels and A-Levels: O-Levels in five subjects with a minimum Average Grade Point of 2.5 and A-Levels in two subjects with a minimum Average Grade Point of 2.0. Only one ‘E’ Grade is acceptable in combined O & A-Levels. (Scale A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2 & E=1)

    3. OR

  3. US High School Diploma, IB Diploma Program (IB-DP) or equivalent
  4. Students with academic qualifications other than the above need to contact Admissions Office to check their eligibility.
  5. Foreign Applicants: candidates must have a minimum avg 60% marks in Grades 10th and 12th.
